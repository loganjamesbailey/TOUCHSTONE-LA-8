Voice mode: it holds your voice for you

Voice mode does the three things a great engineer does by hand: level — a bidirectional rider that lifts quiet phrases and catches loud ones inside a window you choose; effort — pushed singing is spectrally different from soft singing, and the Intimacy control pulls a pushed take back toward close-quiet-singing character at full level, or leans a timid take forward; and distance — it knows whether a bass swell means you leaned into your dynamic mic or just room on a condenser, and rides it out.

It learns your voice's baseline, freezes it when you say so, and saves the profile with your session — so it behaves identically on every playback pass.

Six modes

VOICE

Outcome-defined vocal engine: level window, effort shaping, mic-aware distance stabilization.

CLEAN

Mathematically exact digital compression. Flat to ±0.05 dB, zero latency, nothing you didn't ask for.

FET

1176-class feedback topology. 20–800 µs attacks, ratio detents 4/8/12/20/All, program-dependent release.

OPTO

LA-2A-class T4 cell with light memory: the two-stage release and the glue.

VARI-MU

Fairchild-class progressive ratio — leans in harder as you push it. Slow, deep, transformer-flavoured.

VCA

dbx/SSL-class RMS detection. Literal controls, near-clean, punchy.

Install

  1. Download and unzip.
  2. Copy Touchstone.component to ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components/
  3. Copy Touchstone.vst3 to ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/
  4. Restart your DAW. In Logic Pro: Audio Units > JamesBailey > Touchstone.
